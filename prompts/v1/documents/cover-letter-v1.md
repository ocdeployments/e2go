## Letter Structure (8 paragraphs)

### Paragraph 1: Applicant Introduction
- Name, nationality, visa classification (E-2 Treaty Investor)
- Opening: establish the applicant's identity and purpose

### Paragraph 2: The Business
- Business name, type, location, nature of operations
- What the business does, target market

### Paragraph 3: Investment Summary
- Investment amount
- Source of funds summary (brief)
- At-risk confirmation (funds committed to enterprise)

### Paragraph 4: Substantiality Argument
- Investment as percentage of total startup cost
- Proportionality argument showing substantiality

### Paragraph 5: Non-Marginality
- Revenue projections
- Hiring plan
- Economic contribution to U.S.

### Paragraph 6: Applicant Qualifications
- Professional background
- Experience and skills
- Active management role
- Directly connect past experience to business needs

### Paragraph 7: Non-Immigrant Intent
- Home country ties (family, property, financial interests)
- Intent to depart U.S. when business ceases or status ends
- This addresses the critical non-immigrant intent requirement

### Paragraph 8: Closing
- Request for favorable consideration
- Professional sign-off
