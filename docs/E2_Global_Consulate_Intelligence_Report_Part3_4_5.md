## Data Points Not Publicly Verifiable

These items require direct embassy contact or experienced local immigration attorney input — they cannot be sourced from public documentation:

1. Per-consulate denial rates by business type — attorney experience only
2. Interview tone descriptions — all labeled "community reported"
3. Whether attorneys are permitted inside specific interview rooms — not consistently published
4. Exact 221(g) administrative processing rates by post — not published by State Dept
5. Frankfurt current email submission address — verify directly at de.usembassy.gov/frankfurt
6. Seoul supplement current version — must be downloaded from travel.state.gov each application cycle

8. Each Mexico consular sub-post specific intake instructions
