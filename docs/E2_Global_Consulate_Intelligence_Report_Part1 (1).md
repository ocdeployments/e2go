### Legend
- ⚠️ Processing suspended or restricted
- 🔄 Data needs verification
- 🗓️ Annual Review Required
- **Volume:** High = 500+ E-2 apps/yr | Medium = 100–500/yr | Low = <100/yr | Unknown = no data
- **Appointment Systems:** CGI Federal | AIS | ustraveldocs.com | usvisa-info.com | AIT (Taiwan)

### Critical Policy Note
As of **September 6, 2025**, third-country national E-2 processing has been eliminated. Applicants must apply at a U.S. consulate in their country of **nationality or legal residence** only. 🗓️ Annual Review Required
