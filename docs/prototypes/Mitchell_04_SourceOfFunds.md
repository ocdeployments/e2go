## FUND SOURCE 2: HOME EQUITY LINE OF CREDIT ($90,000)

### Nature of Credit Facility

The HELOC is secured exclusively by Ms. Mitchell's personal residence — 142 Rosedale Heights Drive, Toronto, Ontario. No business assets secure this credit facility, satisfying the E-2 requirement that investment funds not be secured solely by business assets.
