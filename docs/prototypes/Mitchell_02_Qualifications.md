## Professional Background

Sarah Elizabeth Mitchell brings to her E-2 enterprise over twelve years of progressive experience in human resources management, culminating in her most recent position as Director of People Operations for a Fortune 500 healthcare technology company.

## Employment History

**Director of People Operations** | 2019–Present
*MedTech Solutions Inc., Toronto, Ontario*

As Director of People Operations, Ms. Mitchell oversaw a workforce of 2,400 employees across North American operations. Her responsibilities included strategic workforce planning, talent acquisition, compensation and benefits administration, employee relations, and regulatory compliance with both Canadian and U.S. employment law.

Key accomplishments include:
- Implemented a comprehensive talent management system reducing voluntary turnover by 18%
- Led restructuring initiatives resulting in $2.3 million annually in operational efficiencies
- Established offshore staffing partnerships creating 45 new positions
- Navigated complex multi-jurisdictional HR compliance across Ontario, Quebec, and U.S. operations

**Senior Human Resources Manager** | 2014–2019
*Global Logistics Partners LLC, Toronto, Ontario*

Managed HR functions for a logistics company with 850 employees. Oversaw recruitment, training, benefits administration, and labor relations.

**Human Resources Business Partner** | 2012–2014
*Northern Manufacturing Co., Toronto, Ontario*

Provided HR support for manufacturing operations with 600 unionized and non-unionized employees.
