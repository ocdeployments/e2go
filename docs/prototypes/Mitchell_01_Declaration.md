## BODY

I, Sarah Elizabeth Mitchell, hereby declare the following to the United States Consulate General in Toronto:

I am the principal investor and treaty national applying for E-2 Treaty Investor visa classification. I intend to invest the sum of $175,000 USD in Mitchell Care Services LLC, a Texas limited liability company that I have formed and in which I hold 100% ownership interest.

The investment capital has been lawfully sourced from personal savings in the amount of $85,000 USD and a home equity line of credit in the amount of $90,000 USD. These funds represent my personal assets accumulated through gainful employment as Director of People Operations.

I have executed a Franchise Agreement with Assisting Hands Home Care dated April 22, 2026, establishing my rights to operate an Assisting Hands Home Care franchise in Austin, Texas. I have also executed a Commercial Lease for premises located at 4500 Medical Parkway, Suite 210, Austin, Texas 78756.

I commit to using the investment capital solely for the establishment and operation of the E-2 enterprise. The funds have been transferred to the U.S. business bank account at RBC Bank USA, Austin, Texas.

I affirm that I will engage in the development and direction of the enterprise on a substantially full-time basis. I will not engage in any other business enterprise except as necessary for the proper operation of this E-2 business.

I understand that the E-2 visa is a non-immigrant classification and that my stay in the United States is temporary. I maintain significant ties to Canada, including real estate property located at 142 Rosedale Heights Drive, Toronto, Ontario, and family members residing in Canada.

I certify under penalty of perjury that the foregoing statements are true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief.
