⚠️ What NOT to do
Common mistakes to avoid
These mistakes break the Obsidian Gold design system. Never do any of these.
❌ Navy blue background (#060d1f) — use #0a0a0a obsidian
❌ Teal accent (#0D9488) — use #C9A84C gold
❌ Playfair Display headings — use Cormorant Garamond
❌ Glassmorphism with blur — NO blur effects in Obsidian Gold
❌ Rounded corners on buttons/inputs — use sharp edges (radius: 0)
❌ White card backgrounds — always #0a0a0a
❌ Inter/Roboto for body text — use DM Sans
❌ Blue or purple gradients — never use these
