⚠️ What NOT to do
Common mistakes to avoid
These are the mistakes that make e2go look generic or broken. Never do any of these.
❌ White or light grey option buttons on dark backgrounds — always use glass
❌ Question text as a faint watermark — always fully opaque foreground text
❌ Blue progress bars or blue accent colors — always teal #0D9488
❌ Display notes as large white cards — always small dim helper text
❌ "[nationality]" or "[home country]" as literal text — always replaced dynamically
❌ Inter, Roboto, or system fonts — always Playfair Display + DM Sans
❌ Purple gradients, generic SaaS aesthetics, Bootstrap-era styling
❌ Placeholder footer text like "The American Dream Edition"
